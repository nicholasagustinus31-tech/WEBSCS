# Sinergi Cipta Selaras Website

Static multi-page website for PT Sinergi Cipta Selaras, a media representation company. The experience is optimised for international clients with a modern, professional aesthetic and subtle motion.

## Pages
- `index.html` – Home overview with company positioning and performance metrics.
- `about.html` – Company history, mission, and approach.
- `people.html` – Leadership and team philosophy.
- `media.html` – International media partners represented by SCS.
- `partners.html` – Extended partner ecosystem.
- `case-studies.html` – Highlighted campaigns and forum participation.
- `contact.html` – Office information and enquiry form.
- `sso.html` – Single sign-on entry point for clients and collaborators.

## Design System
- **Primary colour:** `#670e16`
- **Secondary colour:** `#036f6d`
- **Font:** Lucida Fax with serif fallbacks
- Clean layouts with shadows, no gradient backgrounds, and scroll-triggered animations defined in `assets/css/style.css` and `assets/js/main.js`.

## Usage
Open any of the HTML files in a web browser. All assets are bundled under `assets/` for easy hosting on static site services.
